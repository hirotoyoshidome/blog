I’m Starting a Tech Blog

It has been eight years since I began working on web service development.

I’ve tried keeping a tech blog several times before, but it was hard to stay consistent and the posts eventually stopped.

Everything that appears before this article is something I saved from those attempts. I’m publishing them here as a reminder to myself to keep going.

So, what’s the spark this time? I’m restarting the tech blog because I’m about to transition from a full-time employee to a freelancer.

My last job was at a startup, so I rarely had the bandwidth to build something of my own; even when I wanted to, I never managed to take action.

But the moment I decided to go independent, a wave of anxiety hit.

Realizing how important it is to keep sharing information, I decided to launch this blog again.

With the help of generative AI, I’m also making the content available in English.

What I’ll Be Writing About

My main battlefield is backend web development and the cloud, especially AWS.

I’ve been immersed in both areas ever since I started working as an employee.

In addition to web technologies, I want to write about the topics I’m passionate about, like search, recommendations, and data analytics.

I’d also love to cover the generative AI trends that are making headlines.

I might even share the bits of engineering philosophy that matter to me as a developer.

In Closing

This post is largely a statement of intent, but from here on out I plan to focus on technical topics.

That’s all for now.

Thank you for reading !!
